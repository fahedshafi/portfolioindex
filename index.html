About Myself

I'm a passionate video editor working with After Effects 2024, specializing in creating minimalistic yet eye-catching edits that leave a lasting impression. My style focuses on clean transitions, smooth pacing, and a sharp visual aesthetic that fits perfectly across platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and reels. From cinematic montages to fast-paced reels or aesthetic mood edits, I can handle a wide range of styles. I always aim to bring creativity, emotion, and clarity into every frame. Whether it's a brand video, a content creator reel, or a personal project, I give it my full attention and polish. Let's create something that not only looks good — but feels powerful too.

Services

I provide high-quality After Effects editing services including cinematic montages, social media reels, VFX transitions, typography animations, motion tracking, and visual enhancements. Whether you're a content creator, brand, or business — I can transform raw footage into polished, eye-catching videos optimized for impact.

Charges

I charge $5–$20 for a 60-second video — depending on the complexity of the edit, effects, and structure involved. For long-form edits (over 60 seconds or YouTube-style videos), we can discuss pricing in detail through a call or chat to make sure everything fits your needs and expectations.
